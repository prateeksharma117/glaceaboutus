About Us

Glance is a platform for people to connect and talk to strangers from all over the world through video call. Our goal is to bring people from different cultures, backgrounds, and perspectives together to foster meaningful conversations and experiences.

We believe that human connection is one of the most important things in life and we strive to provide a safe and secure environment for people to make new connections and broaden their horizons. We value diversity, respect, and tolerance and we do not tolerate any form of hate speech or discrimination.

We are a team of dedicated individuals who are passionate about making a positive impact in the world. We are constantly improving our platform to provide the best possible user experience and to ensure the safety and security of our users.

If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us. We would love to hear from you.
